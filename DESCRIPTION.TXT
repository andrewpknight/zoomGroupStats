Package: zoomGroupStats
Title: Analyze Text, Audio, and Video from Zoom Meetings
Version: 0.0.0.9000
Authors@R: person("Andrew", "Knight", email = "knightap@wustl.edu", role = c("aut", "cre"))
Description: zoomGroupStats provides utilities for processing and analyzing the files that are exported from a recorded Zoom Meeting. This includes analyzing data captured through video cameras and microphones, the text-based chat, and meta-data. You can analyze aspects of the conversation among meeting participants and their emotional expressions throughout the meeting.
